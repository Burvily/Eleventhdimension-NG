About Us

The Eleventh Dimension Ltd: Championing innovation in construction, engineering, and IT project management.

Who we are

The Eleventh Dimension Ltd is a forward-thinking, multidimensional company established with a vision to redefine the built environment through innovative and sustainable solutions. Officially commencing operations on 11th November 2011 in Nigeria, the company has grown into a respected name in real estate, construction, and consultancy, supported by a strong network of subsidiaries, sister companies, and strategic partners.

As a solutions-driven organization, The Eleventh Dimension Ltd provides a wide range of services tailored to the complex needs of both public and private sector clients. Our core competencies include:

  • Project Management
  • Property Development

With over 15 years of experience in the built environment sector, we have been instrumental in shaping the landscape of Abuja, Nigeria’s Federal Capital Territory. Our portfolio includes commercial and residential developments delivered directly and in partnership with our affiliates. These projects span large-scale property developments, turnkey construction, urban planning, and high-end landscaping. Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability has earned us a reputation for delivering results that exceed expectations. Every project is approached with precision, integrity, and a deep understanding of the local context, while also applying global best practices to ensure long-term value.

We offer a comprehensive suite of services that span the entire lifecycle of real estate and infrastructure development, from inception to long-term asset management:

Technology-Driven Project Execution tools that enhance collaboration, reduce waste, and ensure every project is delivered with unmatched precision, clarity, and control.

Our commitment to sustainability is not an afterthought — it is embedded in our process. We pursue green building practices, resource-efficient systems, and climate-conscious design principles to minimize environmental impact and maximize long-term value.

Through responsible development and ethical business practices, we create projects that are economically viable, socially inclusive, and environmentally regenerative.

As experienced developers and advisors, we help clients and partners identify high-yield opportunities in real estate — from acquisition to development, repositioning, and management. Whether you’re an investor seeking solid returns, a government agency pursuing public infrastructure goals, or a private client building your legacy, we provide the insight, structure, and execution power needed to bring your vision to life.

For over 15 years, The Eleventh Dimension Ltd has been at the forefront of Abuja’s transformation — shaping the Federal Capital’s skyline and redefining what’s possible in Nigerian real estate.

Our work stands as a reflection of our core values: innovation, integrity, excellence, and a deep sense of responsibility to people and place.
